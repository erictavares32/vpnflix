Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using VPNs for streaming.

Which VPN is best for Netflix?

Based on our testing, ExpressVPN and NordVPN consistently provide the most reliable access to Netflix libraries worldwide. ExpressVPN offers slightly better performance for HD streaming, while NordVPN has more server options in certain regions.

Is using a VPN for streaming legal?

Using a VPN is legal in most countries, though it may violate the terms of service of some streaming platforms. While services rarely penalize users beyond blocking VPN access, it's important to be aware of local regulations as a few countries restrict VPN usage.

Will a VPN slow down my streaming speed?

Premium VPNs like ExpressVPN and NordVPN have minimal impact on speed. In some cases, VPNs can actually improve streaming quality by preventing ISP throttling. For best results, connect to a server close to your physical location.

Can I use one VPN on multiple devices?

Yes! Most VPNs allow multiple simultaneous connections. Surfshark offers unlimited connections, while NordVPN allows 6 and ExpressVPN allows 8 devices at once. This lets you stream on your TV, phone, laptop, and more with one account.

Do free VPNs work for streaming?

Most free VPNs struggle with streaming platforms due to limited servers and detection. Windscribe's free plan (10GB/month) is one of the few that works occasionally, but for reliable streaming, a premium VPN is strongly recommended.
